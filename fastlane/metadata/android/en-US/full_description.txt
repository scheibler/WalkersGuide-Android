<p><i>WalkersGuide</i> is a navigational aid primarily intended for blind and visual impaired pedestrians. It calculates routes and shows nearby points of interest. The project consists of an Android client and a server component. The latter performs the route calculation. The map data is provided by <a href='https://www.openstreetmap.org' target='_blank' rel='noopener'>OpenStreetMap</a>, a project to create a free map of the world.</p><p><b>Main features</b></p><ul><li>Calculate routes optimized for blind and visually impaired users</li><li>Explore layout of intersections</li><li>Show nearby POI</li><li>Find train and bus stops and get their next departures</li><li>Simulate any street address</li><li>Create your own favorite points</li></ul><p>The application is fully accessible with Androidâ€™s screen reader Talkback.</p><p>Feel free to request new maps.</p>
